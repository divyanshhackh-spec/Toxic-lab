Health Impact Calculations

Cigarette Equivalent

Estimates pollution exposure relative to cigarette smoking.

Cigarettes = PM2.5 (µg/m³) ÷ 22

Life Expectancy Reduction

Derived from long-term epidemiological research on PM₂.₅ exposure.

((PM2.5 − PMref) ÷ 10) × 0.98 × (Age ÷ 70)

These calculations are estimates and intended for awareness purposes only — not medical diagnosis.
